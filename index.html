Jarvis Chatbot

AI Agent for WhatsApp Business

Overview

Jarvis is an AI-powered WhatsApp Business assistant that helps businesses automate customer conversations, answer frequently asked questions, qualify leads, schedule appointments, and provide intelligent support 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — without human intervention.

The Problem

Small and mid-size businesses receive repetitive customer queries every day. Manually responding to each message is time-consuming, inconsistent, and unscalable.

The Solution

A fully automated conversational agent deployed directly inside WhatsApp. It handles the entire conversation flow intelligently, routes complex queries to a human agent, and maintains context across sessions.

Key Features

  • 24/7 automated customer support
  • Lead qualification and appointment booking
  • Custom FAQ handling with contextual memory
  • Smart message routing logic
  • Zero server downtime since launch

My Role

Sole architect and developer. Designed the entire conversation architecture, AI prompt engineering system, webhook integration, and message routing logic — end to end.
